🐻 Wildlife

  • 25 yards from bison & elk
  • 100 yards from bears & wolves
  • Never feed any wildlife
  • Bison cause most injuries
  • If it reacts, you're too close

🌋 Thermal Areas

  • Stay on boardwalks always
  • Ground can collapse suddenly
  • Hot springs are scalding
  • No swimming in thermals
  • Keep kids close at all times

🗺️ Park Tips

  • Limited cell service
  • Fill gas whenever possible
  • Stay hydrated at 7–8k ft
  • Park entry fee: $35/vehicle
  • Download NPS app offline
